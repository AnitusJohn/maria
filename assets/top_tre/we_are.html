Who we are?

Welcome to the Maria Montessori Teacher Training (MMTT). We are primarily a Teacher Training and Research Institute for Early Childhood Education and Development located in India. Our certified MMTT graduates are qualified to work in Montessori pre-schools and many recognized quality Early Childhood Educational and Care Centers in several countries. Trained graduates can either work as a head teacher or an assistant teacher depending on the course certification. Graduates may also wish to pursue other rewarding occupations working with young children outside a classroom setting. The training and experience is particularly relevant for professionals working with either gifted, ill or special needs children (teachers, parents, psychologists, therapists, nurses and doctors). Parent graduates from many walks of life and many with professional backgrounds equally benefit from our courses while gaining valuable skills and knowledge which importantly supports the development of their own children. In addition, MMTT provides several outreach and interventions programs in our local community (and abroad through research) for children and their families.
