Diploma in Child Psychology

Have you ever wondered why some children are active whereas others are shy? Why do they develop differently from one another? How do they develop their thinking and speaking skills? If Child psychology simply fascinates you, then you are in the right place.

Diploma in International Child Psychology Duration: 1 Year Level: Diploma after 10+2 +3 Study Hours: 450 hours Total Credits: 30 Type: International Diploma Diploma in International Child Psychology is a Diploma level Psychology course. This Child Psychology Diploma is the perfect and most insightful study course to take to gather knowledge about how a child’s mind works and why they behave and react to situations as they do. This course will help to give you a much clearer understanding of the importance of play in a child’s life, how they think and feel and how they develop in terms of language and social interaction. Eligibility Any Degree or its equivalent Syllabus Syllabus of Child Psychology as prescribed by various Universities and Colleges Subject Code Subjects to Study Semester – 1 DCP101 Foundation in Child Psychology DCP102 Ethical Issues in Counselling Psychology DCP103 Physical, Social Development & Attachment DCP104 The Emergence of Mind: Consciousness, Cognition and Language DCP105 Personality and Childhood Disorders DCP106 Aggressive Behaviour and Bullying

Semester – 2 DCP201 Intelligence & Attainment DCP202 Gifted and Talented Children DCP203 Educational Psychology and Children with Special Need DCP204 Child Psychology and Psychopathology DCP205 Child Abuse and Effects DCP206 Child and Adolescent Development
