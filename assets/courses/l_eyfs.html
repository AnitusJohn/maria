Early Years Foundation Stage (EYFS)

Early Years Foundation Stage (EYFS)

The introduction to EYFS teaching training course is a comprehensive course for those who are new to the field as well as those who wish to update their practical skills and knowledge. It covers the EYFS framework in detail, as well as safeguarding practices and learning assessments.

Through this course, you will gain a deeper understanding of the early years foundation stage that applies to both teachers and childminders.

By the end of the course, you will be prepared for a successful career in this rewarding industry, equipped with essential skills and knowledge to effectively support the learning and development of your pupils throughout their early years education.

This course will give you sound theoretical and practical knowledge including children’s rights, child health and well-being, family and cultural contexts, and the principles of inclusive practice. It is made up of both taught and work-based elements through which you can apply your knowledge to real work contexts.

The early years foundation stage (EYFS) sets standards for the learning, development and care of your child from birth to 5 years old.

All schools and Ofsted-registered early years providers must follow the EYFS, including childminders, preschools, nurseries and school reception classes.

Would you like to learn more about the EYFS but don't know where to start?

If yes then this is the course for you. This comprehensive and completely up to date course provides a comprehensive step by step guide to understanding all aspects of the Early Years Foundation Stage (EYFS). It is suitable for those new to the EYFS / Early Years Sector.

The course is an online course and so can be studied any time from your own home- there is no classroom attendance required. Learners can enroll any time and have 6 months to complete the course.

Course Length:
